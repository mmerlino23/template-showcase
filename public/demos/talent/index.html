Frequently asked questions

How does the AI candidate scoring work?

Our AI analyzes each candidate's resume against your job requirements, scoring them on skills match, experience level, and overall fit. The algorithm improves over time as you make hiring decisions, learning your team's preferences.

Can I import candidates from other ATS platforms?

Yes. ApplicantTrack supports CSV bulk import, so you can export from any existing system and import directly. We also support direct integrations with popular job boards and ATS platforms on Pro and Agency plans.

Is my candidate data secure?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We are SOC 2 compliant and GDPR-ready. Your candidate data is never shared with third parties or used for training AI models.

How many team members can use ApplicantTrack?

All plans include unlimited team members. The Starter plan includes 1 pipeline, Pro includes unlimited pipelines, and Agency adds API access and white-label options for managing multiple clients.

Can I try ApplicantTrack before committing?

Our Starter plan is completely free with no credit card required. You get 1 pipeline, 50 candidates, and basic scoring. Upgrade to Pro or Agency anytime as your hiring needs grow.
