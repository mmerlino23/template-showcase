Treatment Overview

At Luxe Med Spa Miami, every treatment is performed by board-certified providers using FDA-approved products and the latest technology.

Our Brickell location offers a serene, luxury environment where you can relax while receiving world-class aesthetic care. We combine medical expertise with an artistic eye to deliver natural-looking results that enhance your unique beauty.

During your initial consultation, our team will assess your goals, review your medical history, and create a personalized treatment plan designed to achieve the results you want. We believe in transparency with pricing and realistic expectations.

Who Is This For?

This treatment is ideal for clients looking to refresh their appearance, address specific skin concerns, or maintain their results from previous treatments. Whether you are new to aesthetics or a returning client, our providers will customize the approach to your skin type and goals.

We welcome clients of all ages and skin types. Our consultations are complimentary and designed to help you understand which treatments will deliver the best results for your individual needs and budget.

Luxe Med Spa Miami is conveniently located at 1200 Brickell Ave Suite 400, Miami FL 33131, with flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends. Call (305) 555-0199 to book.

What to Expect

Your visit to Luxe Med Spa Miami is designed to be a relaxing, premium experience from start to finish. Here is what you can expect:

  • Complimentary consultation with a board-certified provider to discuss your goals
  • Personalized treatment plan with transparent pricing and no hidden fees
  • Premium products from trusted brands including Allergan (Botox), Galderma (Restylane), and SkinMedica
  • Post-treatment care instructions and follow-up appointments to ensure optimal results
